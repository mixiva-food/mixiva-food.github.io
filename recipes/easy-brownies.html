These easy brownies are rich, fudgy, and full of chocolate flavor. Made with simple pantry ingredients and one bowl, they are perfect for beginners.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. In a bowl, mix melted butter and sugar until combined.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla extract, then mix well.
  4. Stir in cocoa powder, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  5. Mix until smooth and no dry spots remain.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased baking pan.
  7. Bake for 25–30 minutes or until set.
  8. Let cool before cutting into squares.
