Avocado toast is a quick, healthy, and delicious meal made with ripe avocados and toasted bread. It’s perfect for breakfast, brunch, or a light snack and takes only minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt, to taste
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • 1 tsp lemon or lime juice (optional)
  • Olive oil (optional)

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread slices until golden and crispy.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth or slightly chunky.
  4. Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice if using.
  5. Spread the avocado mixture evenly over the toasted bread.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy.
