This classic grilled cheese sandwich is crispy on the outside and perfectly melty inside. It’s quick to prepare, beginner-friendly, and ideal for breakfast, lunch, or a simple snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your favorite)
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each bread slice.
  3. Place one slice of bread in the pan, butter side down.
  4. Add the cheese slices on top of the bread.
  5. Cover with the second bread slice, butter side up.
  6. Cook until the bottom is golden brown.
  7. Flip carefully and cook until the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from the pan, slice, and serve hot.
