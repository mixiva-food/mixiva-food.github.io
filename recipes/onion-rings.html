These crispy onion rings are golden on the outside and tender on the inside. Made with simple ingredients, they are perfect as a snack, appetizer, or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1 cup milk
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Peel the onions and slice them into rings.
  2. Separate the rings and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, mix flour, baking powder, salt, and paprika.
  4. Gradually add milk and whisk until a smooth batter forms.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  6. Dip each onion ring into the batter, coating well.
  7. Carefully place the rings into the hot oil.
  8. Fry until golden and crispy on both sides.
  9. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  10. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
