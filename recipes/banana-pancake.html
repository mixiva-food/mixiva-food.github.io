These banana pancakes are soft, fluffy, and naturally sweet thanks to ripe bananas. They are easy to make, perfect for beginners, and ideal for a quick breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 ripe banana (mashed)
  • 1 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp melted butter or vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mash the ripe banana until smooth.
  2. Add the egg, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  3. In another bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture.
  5. Mix gently until just combined. Do not overmix.
  6. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  7. Pour small amounts of batter onto the pan.
  8. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
  9. Serve warm with honey, syrup, or fresh fruit.
